Client Communication & Catching Strategy – Muhammad Ahmad

Part 1: Excellent Communication Skills with Clients
---------------------------------------------------

1. Listening First
- Understand client’s pain points before offering solutions.

2. Clear & Simple Language
- Avoid jargon. Explain tech in easy terms.

3. Confidence + Clarity
- Speak with authority and structure your thoughts.

4. Follow-Up
- Send summary emails after meetings.
- Keep communication consistent.

5. Empathy
- Show genuine interest in solving their problems.

Templates:

Discovery Call:
“Thanks for sharing your challenges. Based on what you said, I believe a CI/CD pipeline can reduce your deployment time by 40%. Shall I send a quick proposal?”

Follow-Up Email:
“Hi [Client Name], just checking in to see if you had a chance to review the proposal. Happy to clarify anything!”
