## Key Formal Phrases

### Bonjour (بونژوغ) - "bon-zhoor"
Most common formal greeting. Use from morning to evening with strangers, elders, and in professional settings.  
**Example:** "Bonjour, Monsieur" (Hello, Sir)

### Comment allez-vous? (کو ماں تالے وو) - "koh-mahn tah-lay voo"
Formal way to ask "How are you?"  
**Response:** "Je vais bien, merci" (I'm well, thank you)

### Enchanté/Enchantée (آں شاں تے) - "ahn-shahn-tay"
"Pleased to meet you" - Used at first meetings.  
- Enchanté (masculine)
- Enchantée (feminine)

### Au revoir (او رُہ ووار) - "oh ruh-vwahr"
Standard formal goodbye.  
**Example:** "Au revoir, Madame"

## Formal Conversation Example

**Person A:** Bonjour, Monsieur. Je suis Marie Dupont.  
**Person B:** Bonjour, Madame. Enchanté.  
**Person A:** Comment allez-vous?  
**Person B:** Je vais très bien, merci. Et vous?  
**Person A:** Très bien, merci.

**Leaving:**  
**Person A:** Au revoir et bonne journée!  
**Person B:** Merci. À bientôt!

## When to Use Formal Greetings?

### ✅ Use With:
- Strangers
- Elderly people
- Teachers/Professors
- Bosses/Authorities
- In shops/restaurants
- Professional settings
- First meetings

### ❌ Don't Use With:
- Close friends
- Family
- Children
- Casual settings

---

## Rules

1. **Always use "vous"** (formal you), not "tu"
2. **Add titles:** Monsieur/Madame
3. **Use complete phrases:** "Bonjour" not "Salut"
4. **Proper goodbyes:** "Au revoir" not "Ciao"

---

## Time-Based Greetings

- **Morning to Evening:** Bonjour
- **Evening (6 PM+):** Bonsoir
- **Night (leaving/sleeping):** Bonne nuit

---

**Golden Rule:** When in doubt, use "Bonjour, Monsieur/Madame" ✅
