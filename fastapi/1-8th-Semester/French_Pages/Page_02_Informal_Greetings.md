## French Cheek Kisses (La Bise)

Physical greeting with air kisses on cheeks:
- **Paris:** 2 kisses (left cheek first)
- **Southern France:** 3 kisses
- **Use with:** Friends & family only
- **Don't use with:** Strangers or professional settings
