## üìã **Quick Application Strategy**

### **Step 1: Preparation (1-2 days)**
- **Resume**: 1-page, DevOps-focused
- **GitHub**: 2-3 infrastructure projects
- **LinkedIn**: Optimized with startup keywords

### **Step 2: Application Approach**
1. **Direct Website Application**: Always do this first
2. **LinkedIn Connection**: Connect with hiring managers
3. **Email Follow-up**: After 3-4 days if no response
4. **Referral**: Ask in DevOps communities for connections

### **Step 3: Interview Process (Typical)**
- **Round 1**: HR/Culture fit (30 min)
- **Round 2**: Technical interview (1 hour)
- **Round 3**: CTO/Founder chat (30 min)
- **Total Timeline**: 1-2 weeks for interviews

## üí° **Pro Tips for Startup Applications**

### **What Startups Look For:**
- **Growth Mindset**: Willingness to learn and adapt
- **Ownership**: Taking responsibility for projects
- **Pragmatism**: Simple solutions over complex ones
- **Speed**: Ability to move fast and iterate

### **What to Highlight in Applications:**
- **Startup Experience**: Even if just side projects
- **Learning Agility**: How you pick up new technologies
- **Problem-Solving**: Real examples of fixing issues
- **Collaboration**: Working with small teams

### **Red Flags to Avoid:**
- **Rigid Processes**: Insisting on complex procedures
- **Big Corp Mentality**: Need for extensive documentation
- **Slow Decision Making**: Taking too long to respond
- **Technology Purism**: Insisting on specific tools only
