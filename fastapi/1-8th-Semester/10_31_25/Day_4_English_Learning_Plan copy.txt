--------------------------------------------------
We use the future tense to talk about actions that will happen later.

- **"will"** is used for decisions made at the moment of speaking or predictions.
  Example: I will deploy the update tonight.

- **"going to"** is used for planned actions.
  Example: I am going to monitor the logs after lunch.
