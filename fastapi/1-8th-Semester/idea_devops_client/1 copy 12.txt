Purpose:
Track cold email outreach, follow-ups, and client responses for DevOps, AWS, and development services.

Columns:

1. Client Name
   - Full name of the person you're contacting

2. Company Name
   - Startup or business name

3. Email Address
   - Verified email ID

4. LinkedIn Profile
   - Optional, for cross-reference

5. Industry
   - Fintech, SaaS, E-commerce, etc.

6. Outreach Date
   - Date when the first email was sent

7. Email Status
   - Sent / Opened / Replied / Bounced

8. Follow-Up 1 Date
   - Date of first follow-up email

9. Follow-Up 2 Date
   - Date of second follow-up email

10. Response Received?
    - Yes / No

11. Response Summary
    - Short note on what the client replied

12. Next Action
    - Call scheduled / Send proposal / No response / Closed

13. Notes
    - Any extra info (e.g., client interest, budget, timeline)

Tips:
- Use color coding: Green = Replied, Yellow = Opened, Red = No Response
- Use filters to sort by status or industry
- Update weekly to stay on top of leads
