Cold Emailing – How to Get Real Client Emails

1. LinkedIn + Email Finder Tools
- Find target clients on LinkedIn (CTOs, Founders)
- Use tools:
  - https://hunter.io
  - https://apollo.io
  - https://snov.io
  - https://clearbit.com

2. Company Websites
- Visit “Contact Us” or “Team” pages
- Look for direct emails like john@startup.com

3. Email Patterns
- Use common formats:
  - firstname@company.com
  - firstname.lastname@company.com
  - contact@company.com

4. Scraping (Advanced)
- Use Python + BeautifulSoup or Scrapy
- Use APIs from Hunter or Apollo

Note: Always personalize emails and avoid spam. Build trust first.
